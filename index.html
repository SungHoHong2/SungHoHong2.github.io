Turning point of my life

When I was studying design in some prestigious university in Korea,
I have to admit, life was very easy at then.
Just follow the rules and you will end up working in a prestigious company.

I thought that was the answer, and studied hard all the way to the path.
At the point when I was a fresh employee in the job market,
I begin to question why I started studying art in the first place.

It may sound like odd but ...
I wanted to pursue the Art by using the tools that no artists have ever used before.
I wanted to surpass the paints, brushes, and software design tools that artists have already tried countless times.
I want to use ingredients like high performance computing, and embedded systems to pursue my creative endeavor

That's the reason why I switched to Computer Science
And as a novice in the field of Computer Science, the job market was kicking my ass ever since Life is difficult since I have to start from the very beginning.
The path to working in good companies are closed since I switched my career to computer science.
I do regret sometimes, especially when I have to meet up with my friends who are already seniors in some organization

But my past works and small acheivements give me the courage to go on.
